# Decision Log

Decisions are extracted by `/decision-log` or logged manually. Each entry captures what was decided, why, and by whom.

## Format

```
### [Decision Title]
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Decision**: [What was decided]
- **Context**: [Why — 2-3 sentences]
- **Alternatives considered**: [What else was on the table]
- **Stakeholders involved**: [Who was involved]
- **Implications**: [What changes]
- **Review date**: [Date or N/A]
- **Source**: [Slack channel / email thread reference]
```

(Run `/decision-log` after key meetings to populate this file.)
